HTGM v2 has officially crossed **150+ hours of training**, completed **17 sessions**, and the training journey has now continued for **24 days**.

## HTGM v2 Hindi LLM Progress

### Project Details

- Model Name: HTGM v2
- Type: Hindi Large Language Model (Hindi LLM)
- Architecture: GPT-style Transformer
- Parameters: 163.4M+
- Sequence Length: 2048
- Vocabulary Size: 100,000
- Dataset Size: ~41GB
- Dataset Source: AI4Bharat Sangrah + Manual Cleaning
- Training Platform: Kaggle
- GPU: 2x T4
- Tokenizer: Hugging Face BPE-based Tokenizer

## New AI Research Project – Smell AI

## What is Smell AI?

Smell AI is a new experimental AI model that will try to understand:

### If a person smells something…

- How will they feel?
- Will it feel pleasant?
- Will it feel uncomfortable?
- Will it create calmness, happiness, stress, or discomfort?

The goal is to help AI understand smell-based emotional reactions.

This opens a completely different research direction beyond text and vision models.

---

## Current Status of Smell AI

- Research officially started
- Small initial experiment completed successfully
- Still in very early stage
- Much more work remains

This is only the beginning.

---

## Why Smell AI Matters

Most AI models today focus on:

- Text
- Images
- Voice
- Video

But real human experience includes smell, emotion, memory, and feeling.

Smell AI explores that missing dimension.

This is difficult research — but worth building.
